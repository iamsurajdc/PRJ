The Honest Woodcutter
A poor woodcutter accidentally dropped his axe into the river. He was very upset, as he had no money to buy another axe. Just then, a fairy appeared in the river holding out a golden axe to him. “I found this axe in the river. Is this yours?” The fairy asked him. “That’s not mine,” replied the woodcutter. The fairy then brought out a silver axe. The woodcutter disowned this one too. Finally, the fairy held out a wooden axe. Smiling with happiness, the woodcutter said, “That’s mine. Thank you very much.” The fairy felt very happy seeing his honesty and thought that such honesty deserved a reward. She gave the poor man all the three axes and disappeared.
