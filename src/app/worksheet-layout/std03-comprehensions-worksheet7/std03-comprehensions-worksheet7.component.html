True Wealth

Once upon a time, there lived a very rich and wealthy man in a small town. He led a luxurious life. He always boasted about his wealth to his friends and relatives.

His son was studying in a distant city and he had returned home for vacation. The rich man wanted to show off to his son how rich he was. But his son wasn’t fond of any luxurious lifestyle. However, the rich man wanted to make his son realize that his lifestyle was extremely rich and that poor people suffered a lot. He planned to visit the entire town to show him the life of the poor people.

The father and the son took a chariot and visited the entire town. During the visit, the poor people showed a lot of respect to the rich man. The father saw that his son was very quiet after seeing the poor people suffer, due to lack of facilities.

The rich man asked his son, “Dear boy, how was the trip? Did you enjoyed it?” “Yes my dad, it was a great trip with you,” the son replied. “So, what did you learn from the trip?” the father asked. The son was silent. “Finally, you have realized how the poor suffer and how they actually live,” said the father.

“No father,” replied the son. He added, “We have only two dogs, they have ten dogs. We have a big pool in our garden, but they have a massive bay without any end! We have luxurious and expensive lights imported from various countries, but they have countless stars lighting their nights. We have a house on a small piece of land, but they have abundant fields that go beyond the horizon. We are protected by huge and strong walls around our property, but they bond with each other and surround themselves with their fellow beings. We have to buy food from them, but they are so rich that they can grow their own food.”

The rich father was stunned and speechless, on hearing his son’s words. Finally the son added, “Dad, thank you so much for showing me who is rich and who is poor. Thank you for letting me understand how poor we really are!”
