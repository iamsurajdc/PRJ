THE FOOLISH DEER
One day, when a deer was grazing in a jungle, some hunters arrived there and shot an arrow at it. The poor animal got scared and hid behind a bush. The hunters started searching for it. The deer couldn’t control its patience and started nibbling the leaves of the bush. The hunters heard the rustling sound of the leaves. One of them saw the deer behind the tree and shot an arrow at it and the deer fell down dead. So, it’s always said at the time of danger, one should keep patience and behave intelligently.
