Click in the circles before the sentences to colour them, that answers the questions with the correct use of Prepositions.

1)
Where are Albert’s shoes?
a
Albert’s shoes are beside the door.
b
Albert’s shoes are at the door.
2)
When did Mr. Jason leave the office?
a
Mr. Jason left the office since he finished his work.
b
Mr. Jason left the office after he finished his work.
3)
Where did the horse gallop?
a
The horse galloped through the jungle.
b
The horse galloped along the jungle.
4)
Where did Janet put the flowers?
a
Janet put the flowers on the vase.
b
Janet put the flowers in the vase.
5)
Where does Bob live?
a
Bob lives at New York.
b
Bob lives in New York.
6)
What is the mouse doing?
a
The mouse is running away from the cat.
b
The mouse is running at the cat.
