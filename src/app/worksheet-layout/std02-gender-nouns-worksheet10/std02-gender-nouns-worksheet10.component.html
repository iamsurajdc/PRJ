A Farm Visit

The cows grazed in the fields while the bulls rested lazily in the shed. The cock and the hen played about near their coop. We were fortunate to see a peacock spread its feathers. A mare had just given birth to a foal. A fat pig oinked in the background, whereas the ewe bleated away. As we moved further we could hear the humming of the bees and drones. There were many visitors like us at the farm so it was such a noisy place. All of us came back home tired and exhausted.
