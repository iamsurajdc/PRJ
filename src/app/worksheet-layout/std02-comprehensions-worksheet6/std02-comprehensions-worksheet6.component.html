The Three Different Fishes

Three fishes lived together in a lake. Although the three were together, they were very unique. They had contrasting characters and argued over small things. Still, they were happy.The three grew up into bigger fishes.

The first fish always dwelled in the past and was very lazy. It didn’t believe in preparing for the future. If you can find something that is quite opposite to the phrase, ‘Prevention is better than cure’, it could be the first fish.

The second fish lived for the present. It was a little bit wise and made some good decisions at the last minute.

The third fish was an intelligent one. It usually thought a lot, made a wise decision, always carried smart and interesting ideas and suggestions to live happily and safely.

One day, as the three were playing in the lake, the third fish heard two fishermen talking about fishing. It called the other two fishes to hear what they spoke. The first man said, “I have heard about this lake. It has some big fishes. Why can’t we enjoy our time fishing here?” The second man replied, “Yeah that’s a good idea! I would love to catch and cook a fresh fish. We’ll come here tomorrow noon.” And they left.

The Three Different Fishes

The third fish told the other two fishes, “See they are planning to catch us. We should go to some other place to live safely. I have already found a route that will take us to the nearby canal and we may then reach a new lake.”

The second fish told, “Yeah I too heard them. But they may not return or may end up fishing at a distant place. We can think about it tomorrow when we see them.”

The first fish said, “Oh! Let’s just ignore it. Let us have some rest now.”

The third fish, the intelligent one, moved to the nearby lake through a secret way alone, as the other two fishes denied accompanying.

Next day, the two fishermen arrived. The second fish saw them fishing and planned for an escape. When it was caught in the fishing net, it acted dead. The fishermen threw it back into the lake and it escaped. The first fish, the lazy one had no idea about the presence of the fishermen. Very soon it was caught and before it could think about how to escape, it was put into the basket and lost its life in a few minutes.
