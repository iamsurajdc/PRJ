KIND JUMBO
Jumbo, a big elephant, was very kind and caring towards others. One day, he saw a little boy crying. Jumbo went to him and asked him the reason for crying. The boy replied, “I was carrying water to my home, when I noticed a hole in my bucket. All the water had been drained out. Now how will I take water to my home?” Jumbo consoled the boy and thought of a plan. He filled his trunk with water and went along with boy to his home. There he poured the water from his trunk into another bucket. The boy felt very happy and gave Jumbo bananas to eat.
